# Class-Lens#
Student Attendance Analytics Dashboard featuring data cleaning, KPI tracking, attendance trends, and branch-wise performance analysis and 
visualization of 32K+ attendance records across 800 students and 4 branches of my college, which are recorded in the Google Sheets.

 Project Overview
A comprehensive analytics dashboard engineered to analyze and visualize student attendance patterns across a multi-branch educational institution. 
The dashboard processes 32,000+ attendance records spanning 800 students across 4 branches and 29 subjects, enabling data-driven institutional intervention strategies.
Key Achievement: Identified attendance performance variations (83%-86.50%) that enable targeted interventions and early identification of at-risk students.
